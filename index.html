Why use Nyra

Flexibility

Nyra integrates with your existing health/fitness/nutrition apps and wearables, so you can use it with your existing tools and stop using isolated data.

Integrated

Custom

The app is powered by ML models that learn and adapt to your body and habits. Recommendations are personalized to you, not a one-size-fits-all approach.

Prevention

Nyra translates raw data into daily and weekly nudges: sleep goals, glucose goals, activity suggestions, food timing guidance, stress mitigation strategies, hydration cues.
